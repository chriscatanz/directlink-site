How do I process a wire transfer over $10,000?
For wire transfers over $10,000: 1) Verify customer identity with two forms of ID, 2) Complete CTR filing in BSA module, 3) Obtain supervisor approval, 4) Use Fed reference for same-day processing. Cutoff is 4pm EST.
📄 Source: Wire Transfer Procedures v4.2

What's the escalation path for suspected fraud?
Suspected fraud escalation: 1) Document incident in CRM immediately, 2) Notify Fraud Team via ext. 4500 or fraud@bank.com, 3) Place temporary hold if over $1,000, 4) Do NOT contact customer until Fraud Team advises. SAR filing within 24 hours if confirmed.
📄 Source: Fraud Response Protocol 2024

What are the eligibility requirements for our HELOC?
HELOC eligibility: Minimum 680 credit score, max 80% combined LTV, property must be primary residence, 2 years employment history, DTI under 43%. Minimum line is $10,000, maximum $500,000. Appraisal required for lines over $250K.
📄 Source: HELOC Product Guide 2024

What BSA training is required for new tellers?
New teller BSA requirements: Complete BSA Fundamentals (4 hrs) within first week, CTR filing certification within 30 days, annual refresher training each January. Quiz score must be 85% or higher. Training tracked in LMS under Compliance tab.
📄 Source: BSA Training Requirements 2024

How should I handle a complaint about unauthorized charges?
Unauthorized charge complaints: 1) Express empathy and document details, 2) Initiate Reg E dispute in core system, 3) Issue provisional credit within 10 business days, 4) Provide written acknowledgment within 5 days. Investigation must complete within 45 days (90 for new accounts).
📄 Source: Reg E Dispute Procedures

Understanding Token Pricing

What is a token?

A token is a small chunk of text that the AI reads and writes. On average, a token is 3-5 letters or characters, so one word is usually 1-2 tokens.

"What's the routing number ?"

↑ This question is 5 tokens

📥 Input Tokens

Everything sent to the AI: your questions plus the documents used to find accurate answers.

📤 Output Tokens

Everything the AI writes back: the answers, citations, and explanations you receive.

FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can we get started?

Most institutions are live within an hour. Upload your documents, set permissions, and you're ready to go.

Where does our data go?

Nowhere. Compass runs in a private cloud infrastructure. Your data never leaves your control or touches third-party AI services.

How does Compass prevent hallucinations?

Every answer is grounded in your uploaded documents with source citations. If the answer isn't in your content, Compass says so.

What file formats are supported?

PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and plain text. We're adding more formats based on customer feedback.

Is there per-seat pricing?

No. We believe everyone in your organization should have access without licensing headaches. Simple, predictable pricing.

Can we control who sees what?

Yes. Role-based access controls let you define which teams or individuals can access specific document sets.
