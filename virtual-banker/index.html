FAQ

Common Questions

How does Virtual Banker integrate with our core system?

Virtual Banker integrates via secure APIs with major core banking systems including Jack Henry, Fiserv, and FIS. We handle the integration work as part of implementation.

Can members still reach a live person?

Absolutely. Members can say "talk to a person" at any time, or Virtual Banker will automatically route complex requests to your team. You control the escalation rules.

How secure is the authentication?

Virtual Banker uses multi-factor voice authentication and knowledge-based verification aligned with your security policies. All interactions are logged for audit compliance.

What happens after hours?

Virtual Banker operates 24/7. After hours, it handles self-service requests and can take messages or schedule callbacks for items requiring staff assistance.

How long does implementation take?

Most implementations complete in 4-6 weeks, including core integration, voice customization, and staff training. We handle the heavy lifting.
