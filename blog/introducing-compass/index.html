Introducing Compass: Your Fastest Route to Knowledge

December 19, 2025 Ben Nichols, COO & Co-Founder

Community banks and credit unions are navigating an overwhelming knowledge environment: PDFs buried in drives, procedures siloed across departments, and critical info trapped in tenured employees' heads. In our recent webinar, Directlink unveiled Compass, a purpose-built Conversational AI platform that helps frontline and back-office teams instantly find trusted answers, reduce escalations, and stay compliant.

Why Compass?

Ben Nichols, Directlink's COO and Co-Founder, kicked off the session with a clear diagnosis: most institutions are adopting AI tools piecemeal, like ChatGPT or Copilot, without a scalable, governed strategy. That leads to inconsistency, compliance risk, and tool fatigue.

Compass is different. It's built exclusively for financial institutions and comes with the governance, auditability, and role-based access required in regulated environments.

What Compass Solves

The three key challenges Compass addresses:

  • Scattered documentation and hard-to-access institutional knowledge
  • Over-reliance on "go-to" employees or IT to answer questions
  • Inconsistent onboarding and training for new hires

Instead of navigating intranets or emailing for help, Compass allows staff to chat with an AI assistant trained on your internal policies, procedures, and job aids. The AI returns instant, cited answers, helping reduce error, escalation, and training fatigue.

Built for Real-World Use

The platform runs in a private cloud environment, keeping your data isolated and secure. It includes:

  • Guardrails to prevent hallucinations
  • Role-specific access to content
  • Full audit logs of usage
  • Accelerated onboarding tools, including AI-driven training simulations

And Compass doesn't just give answers. It respects what's in scope for each role and signals when it can't respond, prompting escalation or training reinforcement.

How to Get Started

Rather than launching a massive, unfocused rollout, Directlink recommends starting with a pilot. Choose a single role and set of documents, define guardrails, and measure impact in 30 days. Then scale intentionally, team by team, with an internal champion leading each phase.

Want to See Compass in Action?

Whether you're in IT, compliance, operations, or leadership, Compass is designed to meet your staff where they are and help them do more with what they already have.
